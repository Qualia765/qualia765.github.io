There are many risks assosiated with scanning random QR codes

1. It can lead to a 'phishing' website. For example it looks like microsoft sign in, but instead collects you username and password.

2. You can download malware, which if you are not careful could infect your computer.

3. Websites can collect all sorts of tracking info, telling an attacker where you live, and what you interests are.
